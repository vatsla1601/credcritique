cred landing page

cred landing page

#1

The animation in the landing page appears slowly & sometimes does not appear at all. The entire page goes with minimal, basic & a bold theme. This animation makes the page conjusted & loading takes more time. A possible solution to it could be using the arrow icon used in the slider of blog page, pointing downwards.

#2

The brand icons can be made clickable & can show the rewards those companies have offered to the clients. Thus attracting more people.

#3

The text appears for a very short time, making the readibility poor. This section does not meet the reading requirement & is just scrolled down by many customers. No user wants to put effort in reading the content. A possible solution to it, is increasing the time for content appearance or making the page completely static.

cred blog page

cred blog page

#1

The reader is unaware that the header is a slider. So the arrow in the slider/carousal should be visible for a few seconds.

#2

Only "latest stories" requires a heading at the top because this page is the landing page of the blog section. However "credit cards" does not require any heading because there is already a named button for it (at the top) & the rest pages in blogs section as well.

#3

The background of the heading is the first alphabet of it. It makes sense because here we want to emphasize on the heading. However when the customer clicks on it then in the next page the content is more important than the heading that has already been read so alphabet indexing should not be there.

#4

These 3 pages do not have an introductory statement to it though they are equally important as the remaining pages.

#5

Under these 2 topics the images are exactly the same. There is nothing wrong in doing this however it can be considered as an act of laziness. A possible solution to it is if you could add the bank name on the wallet or card to distinguish the images.

#6

The body of the page is left aligned while the footer & header of the page is centre aligned. So it will be better if the design is similar too. "load more" at the botton should not be in a box. It should only be a clickable link with the same font because the ending of the page looks abrupt.

#7

If a page has images with real world theme, suddenly a clip-art image in between disrputs the flow of the page. This has been repeated twice in the blog section.

cred hiring page

cred hiring page

#1

The blog page has the logo name as "the upper deck" but "we're hiring" page has the original logo name. Hiring page is one of the most important pages of a website so a suggestion from my side is if the logo name for "we're hiring" page could be "the lower deck".

#2

While going through the hiring section I felt that in "by type" an internship section should be added. It gives clarity to the people who are applying in the company. On behalf of million of students it would great if there could be a seperate internship section in the hiring page.
